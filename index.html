What's tinkerhub nssce

Who are we?

TinkerHub NSSCE is a student-run organization at NSS College of Engineering (NSSCE) dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and technical skill development. It is a part of the larger TinkerHub network, which aims to provide a platform for students to engage in hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborative learning in fields such as technology, engineering, and entrepreneurship.

OUR MISSION
TO cultivate a thriving maker culture among students in NSSCE to ignite creativity and equip many young makers with the skills to innovate and shape the future.
